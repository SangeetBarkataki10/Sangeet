What is Pythia?

In an ever-evolving music market driven by rapid technological innovation, understanding listeners has never been more crucial — or more challenging. Pythia simplifies this complexity with a powerful market analysis tool powered by Cyanite.ai that transforms chaos into clarity.

Delivering insights on today’s top tracks in a professional, downloadable business report, Pythia empowers A&R scouts, record label executives, artists, songwriters, and producers alike. With detailed market breakdowns and unparalleled data visualizations that are both stunning and intuitive, Pythia equips you with the knowledge to stay ahead in a dynamic industry.

Let the oracle herself divine unto you clarity from chaos.
